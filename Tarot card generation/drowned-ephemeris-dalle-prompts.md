# The Drowned Ephemeris — DALL-E Production Prompts

## Style Guide (reference — already validated via test generation)

**Medium, non-negotiable across every prompt:** the image must read as something
physically pressed, forged, carved, or struck into a real material, like a rubbing
taken from a hammered relief or a die-struck coin. Never painted, never illustrated,
never drawn linework on a flat surface. No brushwork, no soft painterly shading.

**Anti-drift rules (carried over from the Veil Arcana guide):**
- Never include "tarot card," "card frame," "border," or "card back."
- No text, letters, numerals, or sigils depicted (the app overlays title/keyword).
- Square 1:1 composition. One primary visual anchor per card.
- Every prompt ends: *No paint, no brushwork. No text, no numerals, no card frame.
  Square 1:1.*

**The six materials**, described consistently:
- Stone: "dark slate, cracked by pressure and weathered at the edges"
- Brass: "worn brass and bronze, pitted patina, warm aged metal tones"
- Driftwood: "salt-bleached driftwood, natural wood grain, pale silvered grey"
- Obsidian: "polished black obsidian, glossy and faintly reflective"
- Hammered iron: "hammered black iron, pitted from repeated strikes, faint
  rust-orange at raised edges"
- Fulgurite glass: "branching fulgurite glass, rough sandy exterior, smooth pale
  frosted interior" — always add: "the whole form pressed flat and fused into
  the plane of the slab, no depth beyond the surface, not a freestanding
  object, no ground or floor visible" (this tier has drifted into 3D
  sculpture/figurine renders without this clause; see Explorer/Lost/Condemned
  of the Hadal retries below)

**The six blemishes**, described consistently:
- Mend (gold/silver/pewter): "with one fracture filled and repaired by a vein of
  [metal] sitting proud of the surface"
- Mend, driftwood only (burn-seal): "with a split sealed shut by a dark scorched
  char line"
- Scrape: "with three parallel claw-like gouges raked across the surface"
- Warp: "with one area blistered and scorched outward as if heat pushed through
  from behind"
- Wound: "with a deep jagged tear left raw and unrepaired, edges peeled back"
- Strike (Instrument only): "with a branching Lichtenberg lightning-scar spreading
  from its center, charred at the edges, faint glow still visible in the hairline
  cracks"
- Gap (Absence only): "with a portion of the relief simply blank, as if the
  impression never took there at all"

varying by archetype. Material follows the tier default.

jaw, head tilted slightly forward, one foot forward, alert lean (note: "hood
back" reliably generates as an unhooded, bare-faced figure — corrected below).
Lost = head turned aside, hands slightly out. Prophet = head bowed, one hand
raised near the temple. Condemned = fully hooded, head down, weighted stance.
