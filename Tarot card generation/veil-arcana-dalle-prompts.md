# Veil Arcana — DALL-E Image Generation Prompts

## Style Guide (copy into every generation session)

**Baseline style string** (append to every prompt):

> Dark fantasy illustration, oil painting texture, jewel-tone palette of deep amethyst, tarnished gold, midnight indigo, crimson, and viridian. Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting. Single dominant figure or symbol, no text, no letters, no words, no runes, no numbers anywhere in the image. Square composition 1:1. Rich detail in foreground, atmospheric depth in background. Painterly brushwork. Mythological realism.

**Anti-drift rules:**
- Never include "tarot card," "card frame," "border," or "card back" in a prompt — creates card-within-card framing
- No text, symbols, numerals, or sigils depicted in the art — the app overlays card names
- Keep each prompt under 120 words to prevent token scatter
- One primary visual anchor per card (the figure, the object, or the scene — not all three)
- Specify lighting direction explicitly to maintain visual continuity across the deck
